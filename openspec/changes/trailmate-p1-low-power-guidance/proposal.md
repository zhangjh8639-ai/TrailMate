# Change: Add low power hiking guidance

## Why

Low battery is a real hiking safety risk because the phone may be the user's route, map, position, and communication tool. TrailMate already surfaces battery status in field diagnostics, but it does not yet give a concise action sequence when the battery is low or critical.

## What Changes

- Add local low-power guidance when phone battery is low or critical.
- Prioritize manual safety actions: capture/share a final reliable position, reduce screen use, keep offline route context available, connect power bank, shorten/exit the route when critical.
- Keep the copy honest: TrailMate cannot extend battery life, toggle system power settings, guarantee navigation, or provide rescue.

## Non-Goals

- Automatic Android battery saver toggles.
- GPS shutdown, background service policy changes, or power management automation.
- Rescue dispatch, emergency calling, or contact automation.
