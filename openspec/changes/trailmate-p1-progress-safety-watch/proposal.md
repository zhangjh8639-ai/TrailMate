# Change: Add progress safety watch

## Why

TrailMate's hiking value is not just recording movement. When a hiker is moving much slower than the route plan, the risk may be rising: daylight, weather, battery, and return transport windows can all tighten. The route tab should help users notice that pressure early without pretending to diagnose physical condition.

## What Changes

- Add a local progress safety watch that compares elapsed recording time, planned checkpoint timeline, current route progress, and remaining distance.
- Surface caution or alert guidance on the route tab when progress is materially behind plan.
- Recommend manual actions: stop and rest, review body condition and weather, shorten the route, use safe-exit guidance, and share current location when the situation is severe.
- Keep copy conservative: TrailMate does not diagnose fatigue, guarantee safety, or automatically contact anyone.

## Non-Goals

- Medical or fitness diagnosis.
- Automatic route rerouting.
- Emergency dispatch or automatic contact messaging.
- Replacing offline maps, route signs, local warnings, or user judgment.
